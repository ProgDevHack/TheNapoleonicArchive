Napoleon

Visit the places Napoleon Bonaparte conquered.

Italy

A country in southern Europe known for its rich history, art, culture, and iconic landmarks like the Colosseum, Venice's canals, and the leaning tower of Pisa.

Austria

A landlocked country in Central Europe famous for its stunning Alpine scenery, classical music heritage, and imperial architecture in cities like Vienna and Salzburg.

Egypt

A North African country renowned for its ancient civilization, including the Pyramids of Giza, the Sphinx, and the Nile River, as well as its significant historical and archaeological sites.

Russia

The largest country in the world, spanning Eastern Europe and Northern Asia, notable for its diverse landscapes, iconic cities like Moscow, and rich cultural heritage.
